   CYBER SENSE HUB
An AI-Powered Cybersecurity Innovation for Digital Safety; Smart Awareness. Strong  Defense … ..
Overview: Cyber Sense  Hub is an AI-Powered, all-in-one cybersecurity platform
designed to protect students, small businesses, and academic campuses from online scams, digital fraud, account hacking, and cyber threats.
With the rapid growth of digital activities in Nigeria, many users fall victim to phishing links, fake transfers, identity impersonation, cyberbullying, and social engineering attacks due to a lack of awareness and access to cybersecurity tools. Cyber Shield  Hub addresses this gap by providing simple, affordable, and user-friendly cybersecurity solutions.
PROBLEM STATEMENT & PROPOSED SOLUTION
Problem Statement
•	Students frequently fall victim to fake scholarships, job scams, phishing links, and hacked social media accounts.
•	Small businesses and POS operators lose money to fake transfer alerts, impersonation, and account takeovers.
•	Academic campuses lack a central system for reporting, tracking, and responding to cyber incidents.
•	Most existing cybersecurity tools are too complex, expensive, or designed for large organizations.
•	Team name: Secure X
•	College: Chris Flourish School, Lagos State University, and Lagos State University. 


Proposed Solution
Cyber Sense Hub is an AI-powered, all-in-one cybersecurity platform that protects students, small businesses, and campuses by:
•	Detecting scams and phishing attempts
•	Verifying suspicious payment alerts
•	Educating users in simple language
•	Providing a centralized cyber incident reporting system
COMPLETE SOLUTION FLOW
1.	User Input
Users submit suspicious messages, links, screenshots, or reports through the platform.
2.	AI & Rule-Based Analysis
The system analyzes inputs using AI models and known scam patterns.
3.	Threat Detection & Classification
Scams such as phishing, fake transfers, impersonation, and account threats are identified.
4.	User Explanation & Alert
Results are explained in simple, non-technical language with safety advice.
5.	Reporting & Tracking
Incidents are logged to track trends, especially within campuses.
6.	Awareness & Alerts
Users receive alerts about trending scams and cybersecurity tips.

•	Database: SQLite / MySQL
•	Security: Input validation, password hashing (bcrypt), authentication, activity logging
•	Tools: VS Code, Git, GitHub
Implementation Approach
•	Modular system design (AI detection, business protection, campus reporting)
•	Secure data handling and user authentication
•	Simple UI focused on non-technical users
•	Continuous improvement using reported scam data
